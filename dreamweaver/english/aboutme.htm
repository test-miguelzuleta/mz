I was born in New York City to Colombian parents in the summer of 1986. After living in the United States for a couple of years my parents moved back to Colombia, which is where I grew up. At the age of 16, after earning my highschool degree in Bogota, I decided to pack my bags and set off back to America to go to college. After getting my degree in graphic design from Youngstown State University I moved to Belmont, CA where I am currently working as a freelance graphic artist and English/Spanish/Chinese translator. If there is anything you need designed or translated, give me a call and I will help you out!
